L.UX Workshop 1: Reciprocated Learning

“Who dares to teach must never cease to learn.” - John Cotton Dana

On the 2nd of February 2016 we ran our first workshop. A free Introduction to User Experience Design, led by Eliot.

The workshop was, by our expectations, a roaring success: there was a high attendance rate (49 students of 52 RSVP’s), received great feedback and met some pretty awesome people along the way.

As much as it was about us teaching, it was also a learning experience for ourselves. We’ve learnt more about how we work together as a team, about Wellington’s passionate UX community and what they hope to learn.
