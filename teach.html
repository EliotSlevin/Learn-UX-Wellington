Share your Knowledge

Wellington is home to fantastic design talent, and a lot of industry experience. Knowledge like this is hard to acquire, very few people actually understand how design is applied and used in the tech industry.

If you have this knowledge, we’d love to help you share it. We run workshops, lectures, classes, and would love you to help run one - even if you’ve never tried to teach design before.

What we provide

There’s a lot to running an event, and we realise that you are very busy. So we handle all the boring stuff - finding a space, organising a time, marketing to the right people, and make sure that people are interested and keen to attend.

First we figure out what content you could teach, and who it would be aimed at. Would it be a free lecture for 50 people? Or a paid workshops for 10? It all depends on what content you want to teach.

Then we promote the event, asking people to register their interest. Once we have enough people keen to come along, then we organise a time, space, and start marketing. Then you can start giving more thought about the content of the event. We do this because we know you’re busy - and nobody wants to write a whole lecture then realize nobody wants to hear it.

Then the event runs! We handle all organising of tickets / attendees, and are there on the day to set up any equipment you might need, direct people into the room, and make sure it all goes smoothly.

If you have knowledge to share, we want to hear from you.
